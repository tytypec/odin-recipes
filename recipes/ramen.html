Ramen

This is a new twist on some classic ramen.

I make this as a go to "crap I don't have anything to eat at my house."

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 green onion
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg
  • sesame seeds
  • meduim cornstarch slurry
  1. Boil ramen until its soft. Drain and set aside
  2. In meduim sauce pan on meduim heat add butter, sesame oil, and garlic.
  3. When garlic is fragrent lower heat and add soy sauce.
  4. Add some of the slurry reserving some if needed later.
  5. Add in noodles.
  6. Put noodles to side of pan. Lightly scramble egg in other half of pan.
  7. Stir in cooked egg.
  8. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds.
