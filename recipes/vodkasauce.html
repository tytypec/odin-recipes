Vodka Sauce

This is one of my family favorites. We used to go out to dinner to Amici Milano's in Trenton and this dish was a must have.

It wasn't until years later that we were able to replicate this recipie and its much easier than you might think!

  • 1 cup vodka
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 16 oz can of whole tomatoes
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 8 oz pack of panchetta (you can get this from Trader Joes)
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • ~3 oz parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  1. Dice onion into small cubes set aside. Heat meduim sauce pan add oil and panchetta.
  2. After about 3 minutes or panchetta begins to get crispy add onion. Saute onion for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in vodka. Let vodka simmer on meduim heat for 3 minutes.
  4. Smash tomatoes with your hand into sauce pan.
  5. Let tomatoes simmer for about 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in heavy whipping cream.
  7. Add basil and parmesan to taste.
  8. Serve hot over pasta.
